Eric Huffman

Frontend developer and passionate storyteller with nearly a decade of experience creating engaging digital experiences with HTML, CSS and Javascript.

Most people call me Huff

I'm a lifelong Detroiter and Michigan State alum who has been building on the web for nearly a decade. I began with mostly self-teach courses and tutorials and eventually built up the courage to attend tech related networking events. It was clear that I had a steep learning curve ahead of me, but after getting a taste of corporate life through a developer internship at Rock Ventures, I found myself ready to tackle the demands of multiple clients and strict deadlines. After taking some time to work with my hands, I landed a frontend developer position with Xperience Communications and haven't looked back since.
